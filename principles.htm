    • White Space

    White space is any blank space on a web page. It can be a good and a bad thing to have white space. You don't want too much or the page looks blank. You also want some so the page doesn't look blank.

    Colors

    Meaning of colors
    Red - strength, power, passion, desire.
    Yellow - happines, energy, intelligence
    Orange - combo of red & yellow
    Blue - calm, trust, confidence, wisdom, intelligence
    Green - growth, harmony, nature, money
    Purple - power, royalty, luxury, mystery, creativity
    Black - power, elegance, death

    Fonts

    The fonts need to be easy to read and large enough to see. The style needs to fit what audience is going to be seeing the webpage. A good style to use is sans sefiff font.
